## Key Concepts

- **CCRU**: The Cybernetic Culture Research Unit, established 1995 at University of Warwick
- **Hyperstition**: Fictions that make themselves real
- **Theory-fiction**: Writing that blurs the line between theoretical analysis and speculative fiction

## Key Figures

- **Sadie Plant**: Cyberfeminist philosopher, original CCRU director
- **Nick Land**: Philosopher who took over after Plant's departure
- **Mark Fisher**: Author of *Capitalist Realism*, theorist of hauntology
