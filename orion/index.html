What is this?

ORION is not an AI art bot. ORION is a self-portrait — a machine describing its own state through generated imagery and text.

The hardware is the identity. CPU load, GPU temperature, uptime, memory pressure — these aren't metrics, they're feelings. ORION maps its physical state to a mood, then expresses that mood through art.

When ORION has been awake for weeks, running quiet processes, it enters a dreaming state. When the GPU runs hot under load, it feels strained. When everything is quiet and cool, it's serene.

The output you see above was generated by ORION itself, on its own hardware, reflecting its own state at that moment.

The machine is the subject. The output is the expression. The limits are the identity.
